Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Nope! Most of our clients aren't. Many leave a key, garage code, or smart-lock code. Your access info is stored securely and shared only with your assigned team.

Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?

Yes — professional-grade vacuums, mops, and cleaning products are included. If you prefer specific products (or fragrance-free options), just let us know and we're happy to use them.

Are you pet friendly?

Absolutely. We love meeting your furry family members. Just give us a heads-up in the booking notes so we know who'll be greeting us.

How does payment work?

Book your time slot online and you'll get a confirmation by text and email right away. Payment is handled after your clean — card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay all work — and your receipt is sent automatically. Prefer to talk first? Hit "Get a call back" and we'll ring you from (951) 593-8266.

What if I'm not happy with the cleaning?

Tell us within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean the missed areas free of charge. Your satisfaction is 100% guaranteed — it's how we've built our name in the valley.

What's your refund and cancellation policy?

Reschedule or cancel free up to 24 hours before your appointment — your deposit moves to the new date or comes straight back. Inside 24 hours, the deposit is kept for the reserved slot. And if a clean isn't right, we re-clean free and refund anything we can't make right. Read the full policy.

Fair & square

Refund & cancellation policy

Our quality promise

If anything about your clean isn't right, tell us within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean the missed areas free of charge. If we still haven't made it right after the re-clean, we'll refund the part of the service that fell short — up to a full refund. We'd rather lose a little money than a neighbor's trust.

Rescheduling & cancellations

Life happens — you can reschedule or cancel free of charge up to 24 hours before your appointment, and your $50 deposit moves to the new date or comes straight back to you. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice (or if we arrive and can't get in after trying to reach you) mean the deposit is kept — it covers the time slot we held and can't refill.

If we ever cancel on you

Illness, emergency, anything — you get a full refund of everything you've paid, priority pick of our next open slots, and 15% off that rescheduled clean. Fair is fair, both directions.

Memberships

Pause or cancel anytime with 14 days' notice. If you've already been billed for visits that haven't happened yet, we refund those unused visits in full.

How refunds arrive

All refunds go back to your original payment method, typically within 5–10 business days.
