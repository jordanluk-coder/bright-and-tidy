Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Nope! Most of our clients aren't. Many leave a key, garage code, or smart-lock code. Your access info is stored securely and shared only with your assigned team.

Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?

Yes — professional-grade vacuums, mops, and cleaning products are included. If you prefer specific products (or fragrance-free options), just let us know and we're happy to use them.

Are you pet friendly?

Absolutely. We love meeting your furry family members. Just give us a heads-up in the booking notes so we know who'll be greeting us.

How does payment work?

We use Square for secure online payments — pay by card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay. You're only charged after your cleaning is confirmed, and receipts are emailed automatically.

What if I'm not happy with the cleaning?

Tell us within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean the missed areas free of charge. Your satisfaction is 100% guaranteed — it's how we've built our name in the valley.
